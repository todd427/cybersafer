What this tool does

Read this before you begin.

🎭 Scenarios use AI-powered characters that simulate real online threats — phishing, scams, bullying, manipulation. They are designed to be convincing.
🚩 Your responses are analysed to identify when you spot warning signs. Nothing you type is stored, logged, or seen by anyone.
⚠️ Some scenarios contain content that may be upsetting — bullying, manipulation, threatening messages. Content warnings appear before these scenarios.
🔒 No account is needed. No personal data is collected. Sessions are anonymous and temporary.
🛡️ This is a safe environment. Nothing here is real. The goal is to help you recognise these threats before you encounter them.

By continuing you confirm you understand this is an educational simulation.
